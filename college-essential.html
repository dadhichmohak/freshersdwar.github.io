Essential Checklist for Freshers: What to Bring to College?

As you embark on an exciting new chapter of your life, it's crucial to ensure that you have everything you need to make a smooth transition to college, including your hostel accommodation. To help you prepare, we've compiled a comprehensive checklist of essential items to bring along with you. From personal items to study essentials, we've got you covered!

Clothing and Accessories

  • Sufficient everyday outfits, including casual and formal attire (1-2 formal attire)
  • Towels, undergarments, shoes (formal and casual), socks, and sleepwear
  • Belts, handkerchief
  • Seasonal clothing (jackets, sweaters, raincoats, etc.) can bring them whenever you go to your home on vacations
  • Matress, blankets
  • Bedsheet, pillow covers, 1 pillow,curtains

Toiletries and Personal Care Items

  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, and mouthwash
  • Soap, shampoo, conditioner, comb, cream and oil
  • Detergent and washing soap
  • Trimmer/ Shaving Kit
  • Nail cutter
  • Bathroom stuff (bucket, mug, mirror, etc...)

Electronics and Appliances

  • Laptop, mobile
  • Laptop Accessories (mouse, keyboard, etc...)
  • Pendrive or external hard disk [if you find laptop storage is insufficient, take pendrive (min 16 GB) for safety]
  • Chargers and other cables
  • Headphones or earphones
  • larm Clock (especially for night owls)
  • Extension board

Study Essentials

You can buy it at the campus also but if you have extra then come with it.


  • Notebooks, folders, pens, pencils, highlighters, and marker
  • Stapler (stapler pins), scissors, tape
  • Sticky notes and index cards

    • And most important thing

  • Scientific Calculator (maybe branch specific, prefer to buy after consulting with your professors)

Other Items

  • Personal identification documents (ID cards etc.)
  • Some medicines (headache, stomachache, fever, cold, cough etc)
  • Hangers/pins for clothing
  • Sports equipment or hobby-related items (if applicable)
  • Basic utensils (कटोरी, चम्मच, गिलास)
  • Snacks
